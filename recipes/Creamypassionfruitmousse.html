Creamy passion fruit mousse

Ingredients

  • 400g powdered milk.
  • 2packets of passion fruit juice powder or similar.
  • 600ml ice cold water.
  1. Combine all ongredients in a blender.
  2. Mix throughly until a very creamy,thick consitency is achieved.
  3. Chill for 2-3 hours then enjoy.
