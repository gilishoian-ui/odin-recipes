Banana smoothie recipe

Banana smoothie

Ingridients required

  • Two ripe bananas.
  • One cup of milk(almond, dairyoat or soy).
  • Half a cup of plain or vanilla greek yoghurt.
  • One table spoon of honey or maple syrup
  • Half a tablespoon of vanilla extract and a pinch of cinnamon
  • ice cubes(optional)
  1. Pour the one cup of milk and half a cup of of yoghurt into a blender.
  2. Add the banana chunks, sweetner of your choice, vanilla extract and cinnamon to the blender.
  3. Start the blender at low speed and gradually increase, blend the mixture for 45-60seconds.
  4. If tou love it cold add some ice cubes and enjoy your banana smoothie

Creamy passion fruit mousse

Ingredients

  • 400g powdered milk.
  • 2packets of passion fruit juice powder or similar.
  • 600ml ice cold water.
  1. Combine all ongredients in a blender.
  2. Mix throughly until a very creamy,thick consitency is achieved.
  3. Chill for 2-3 hours then enjoy.

Banana pancake

Ingredients

  • One banana.
  • 2 large eggs.
  • pinch of cinammon,honey and vanilla extract.
  1. Smash the bananas until smooth and consistent.
  2. Break the two large eggs in a bowl ad whisk them until fully mixed.
  3. Mix the mashed banana and the whisked egg and whisk the untill fully combined.
  4. Add a pinch of cinammon and vanilla extract and whisk until mixed.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan on medium-low heat and grease it with butter or oil.
  6. Add about 2 table spoons of butter per pancake and make them small for easy flipping.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batch.
  8. Drizzle some honey on top and enjoy.
